Italian Classic

Classic
Margherita
Pizza

SIMPLE • FRESH • ICONIC

4.6 300 kcal 4

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

STEPS

  1. Preheat the Oven

    Set to 475°F (245°C) and let it reach temperature.

  2. Prepare the Dough

    Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.

  3. Assemble the Pizza

    Spread tomato sauce, top with mozzarella and basil.

  4. Bake to Perfection

    Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden.
